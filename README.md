## Project Overview

o	Peak carpenters was established in the year 2000 in Woodlands, Durban.
o	It began as family-owned small business, it received fundings and donations from big public figurers
o	PC was mainly focused on fixing wardrobes, basic woodwork making small benches and simple chairs and it grew to customizing furniture.
o	It expanded bigger projects like renovations, commercial and more services that include cabinetry
.

## Website Goals and Objectives

o	Provide information in detail about services we offer.
o	Promote our products and services.
o	Advertise completed projects and for marketing
o	Build leads from businesses, homeowners, government and financial institutes
o	Create visibility and exposure to draw potential clients
o	Expose our credibility, recommendation and trust.
•	Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
o	Traffic growth, number of website visitors. 
o	Period of time spent on the website
o	Requests and bookings and enquiries
o	Rate of Conversion, visitors becoming clients
o	Completed project views and reviews
o	Project requests from potential clients, large organizations and homeowners
o	Ratings
o	Reviews, recommendations, rankings and ratings
o	Most enquired enquiries
o	Number of bookings and contact reaching.
